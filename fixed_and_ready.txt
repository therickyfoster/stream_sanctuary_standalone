1. Go to twitch.tv/dashboard/settings/stream
2. Copy your Stream Key
3. In Stream Sanctuary:
   - Click "Twitch" platform card
   - Paste stream key
   - Click "Start Capture"
   - Click "Start Streaming"
4. When done, download the file
5. Upload to Twitch as a video

FOR YOUTUBE:
1. Go to youtube.com/live_dashboard
2. Copy your Stream Key
3. In Stream Sanctuary:
   - Click "YouTube" platform card
   - Paste stream key
   - Click "Start Capture"
   - Click "Start Streaming"
4. When done, download the file
5. Upload to YouTube

FOR LIVE STREAMING (Advanced):
Use OBS Studio:
1. Add "Browser Source" in OBS
2. Point to this HTML file (file:///path/to/index.html)
3. Set OBS output to Twitch/YouTube
4. Stream from OBS
This gives you real RTMP streaming!
